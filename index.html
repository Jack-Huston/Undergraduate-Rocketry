Solid Rocket Motor — R Class

I supported integration and test activities for an R-class solid motor, including installation on a test stand for proof pressure operations and field firings. This portion of the work is primarily a show-and-tell story.

Field Testing

Participated in field operations for motor checkout and test runs, supporting procedures, comms, and safety perimeters.

  • Range setup and countdown flow
  • Ground safety and comms
  • Post-test inspection

Failure & Findings

During a subsequent firing, the motor experienced a catastrophic failure. Post-event analysis indicated porous propellant fractured during combustion and obstructed the throat, driving a rapid over-pressure. The back end separated, the casing was ejected, and partially burned propellant remained at the site.

What I documented: failure sequence photos, melted stand components, and debris field. The effort tapered after this event; my role here is represented primarily via imagery and test participation rather than deep design artifacts.
